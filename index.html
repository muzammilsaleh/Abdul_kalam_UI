Tribute to A.P.J Abdul kalam

About A.P.J Abdul Kalam:

APJ Abdul Kalam is a famous name in the whole world. He is counted among the greatest scientists of the 21st century. Even more, he became the 11th president of India and served his country. He was the most valued person in the country as his contribution as a scientist and as a president is beyond compare.


Achievements:

  • India's first Indigenous Hovercraft
  • India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle
  • INCOSPAR Committee
  • Ballistic Missile Project
  • Chief Scientific Advisor and DRDO Secretary
  • Launch Vehicle
  • Integrated Guided Missile Development Program
